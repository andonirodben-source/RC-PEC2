Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

This ontology models semantically the EUDAMED cataloge, including devices, manufacturers, identified risks, incidents and security corrective actions.

Introduction back to ToC

The goal of the ontology is to allow the accurate share of data among manufacturers, regulatory entities, hospitals and patients.
